Our Story

Born from a love for the sea and a passion for authentic Mexican cuisine, El Capitan was founded to bring the taste of the coast to the city streets. Our captain, a third-generation fisherman, hand-selects the freshest seafood every morning.

We believe in simple, powerful flavors. Our recipes are family heirlooms, passed down through generations, combining traditional techniques with a modern twist. From our signature fish tacos to our fiery aguachile, every dish tells a story of heritage, freshness, and dedication.
